FAQ

❀ What time should I arrive at the ceremony?
Doors open at 3:30pm so please arrive then to ensure that there's enough time to park and walk to the ceremony site. The ceremony will begin promptly at 4:00pm.
❀ When should I RSVP by?
Check the RSVP section of our website! :)
❀ Can I bring a plus one?
We can only accommodate the individual(s) named on the invitation envelope. No plus ones unless clearly designated, but we can make exceptions on a case by case basis if you contact us directly.
❀ What's the dress code?
We recommend semi-formal/cocktail attire, but it is not required. Please note that part of the day's festivities is outdoors and may involve walking on lawns, so choose your footwear accordingly! It may also be chilly so please consider bringing layers.
❀ Is there parking for the ceremony/reception?
There is plenty of parking available on site.
❀ Is Wi-Fi available?
Feel free to connect to wi-fi 'nestldown' as some carriers may have poor connection at the venue.
❀ Should I take an Uber/Lyft to the venue?
Sure! Keep in mind that Uber/Lyft do not pick up from Nestldown at night. We will provide a shuttle accommodation to Los Gatos for those who Uber/Lyft. Please fill out this information in the RSVP so we know in advance.
❀ Do you guys have a hotel block?
Yes! Please reserve one with a credit card by March 25th 2020.
❀ Is there a registry?
We will not be setting up a registry for our wedding. We're so lucky to be able to celebrate this day with our family and friends. Thank you!
